Mapping Family Sites

September 24th, 2016

I've been working with Google Maps for years. Here is one I've created that records family sites. Sites such as graves, homes, etc. It contains different layers for different branches of my family. You can find those other layers by clicking on the menu items at the top of the map. I suggest you view in a larger map for the best experience.
